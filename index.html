About Us

Boarding House is an online platform which lets the tourists to enjoy homestay facility in Nepal. It provides tourists a variety of options to book the room according to their needs. It mainly focuses on three factors: safety, hygiene and hospitality of tourist. We also provide various offers to encourage the tourists to revisit the country, helping them to easily understand and adapt to Nepalese culture.

Our Events

Jan 02, 2019 Event
Chaliya folk dance

The Chaliya dance, which used to be popular in all nine districts of the Far Western Nepal and also in Kumaun Gahrwal of India till few years ago, is now on the verge of extinction. So, to prevent from the extinction we conduct a chaliya dance in most of the places of Nepal.

Jan 02, 2019 Event
Sauraha Dance Event

Sauraha is 166 km, south west from Kathmandu. It is situated at an altitude of 150m (492 feet) from the sea level. We conduct a dance in Sauraha where their people dance wearing their cultural dress in order to preserve the rites and rituals of our country.

Jan 02, 2019 Event
Lakhe Nach

Lakhey Dance is one of the most popular dances of Nepal. Performers wearing a Lakhe costume and mask perform dances on the streets and city squares during festivals. The Lakhe stops at major crossroads and market squares to give a performance. During the dance, a small boy is known as Jhyalincha (झ्यालिंचा) taunts the Lakhe making him chase him in anger.
